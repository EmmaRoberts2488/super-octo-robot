Practicing Mindfulness

Mindfulness applies to every aspect of life. It is central to an indivual persons life. Often people lose their sense of autonomy in relationships. Taking time to be mindful every day, be it by yourself or as a couple, it vital to starting your journey off on the right foot.

Always Remember to Communicate

Communication is KEY for a married couple. While dating you probably found that communication was pretty easy most times, or else you probably wouldn't have progressed towards marriage. That being said, in marriage you need to learn to communicate in a healthy and productive ways when one or both of you are having hard days, there is a disagreement, or you feel emotionally far away. Ask your spouse how they preferred to be communicated with in cetain ways. Always ask about how each others' day went. Keep communication at the forfront at your relationship.

Something many married couples forget: GO ON DATES!

Never stop dating your spouse. Plan time together EVERY week to spend time enjoying your spouses company in a planned way. You can stay in your apartment or go out. You can spend money on your date or do something for free. You can make it a double with some other married friends or have it be just you two. But no matter what, keep dating each other!!
